Vegan Pasta

Description

My great-great-grandmother devised this vegan pasta recipe at the turn of the century.

Ingredients

  • Dried pasta of your choice
  • Tomato pasta sauce - homemade or pre-bought
  • Mixed herbs
  • Vegan protein of your choice
  • Chilli powder

Steps

  1. Boil the dried pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Bring the pasta sauce to a simmer and add chilli powder to taste.
  3. Fry up vegan protein and add to the pasta sauce.
  4. Leave the sauce to simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Drain pasta, and serve as above or however your heart desires!
