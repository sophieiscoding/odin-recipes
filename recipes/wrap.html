Description

Quick, easy vegetarian wrap! Great for lunches.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Vegetarian chicken substitute
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken substitute according to package instructions.
  2. Chop the vegetables
  3. Warm the tortilla in the microwave for 10 seconds
  4. Build your wrap, adding sauce to your taste
